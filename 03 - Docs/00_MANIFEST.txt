  I 72.0 = Elbo Rapido (Fast speed) - 14 uses
  I 72.1 = Elbo Medio (Medium speed) - 5 uses
  I 72.2 = Cmd Rot (Rotation mode) - 11 uses
  I 72.3 = Cmd LockTable (CRITICAL: Table lock) - 23 uses ‚ö†Ô∏è
  I 72.4 = Pressostato (Water pressure) - 1 use
  I 72.5 = VFD1_IsRunning (Inverter 1 status) - 11 uses
  I 72.6 = VFD2_IsRunning (Inverter 2 status) - 6 uses
  I 72.7 = VFDDisk_IsRunning (CRITICAL: Disk status) - 9 uses ‚ö†Ô∏è

I 73.x - AXIS SELECTION, DIRECTION & MASTER ENABLE BYTE:
  I 73.0 = Eixo Corte (Z-Axis: Vertical blade) - 7 uses
  I 73.1 = Eixo Translacao (Y-Axis: Horizontal) - 5 uses
  I 73.2 = Eixo Cala (Tilt axis) - 2 uses
  I 73.3 = Eixo Rotacao Mesa (Table rotation) - 16 uses
  I 73.4 = Eixo Rotacao Disco (Disk rotation) - (inherited)
  I 73.5 = Direcao "+" (Positive direction) - 17 uses
  I 73.6 = Direcao "-" (Negative direction) - 17 uses
  I 73.7 = V/S (CRITICAL: Velocity enable) - 10 uses ‚ö†Ô∏è

‚ö†Ô∏è = CRITICAL SAFETY SIGNAL

================================================================================
THREE MOST CRITICAL SIGNALS
================================================================================

‚ö†Ô∏è I 72.3 (Cmd LockTable) - 23 REFERENCES
   Purpose: Lock work table in position during cutting
   When active: Q 7.3 (oil pump) is blocked ‚Üí table cannot move
   Safety: Prevents accidental material movement during blade contact

‚ö†Ô∏è I 72.7 (VFDDisk_IsRunning) - 9 REFERENCES
   Purpose: Indicate cutting disk is actively spinning
   When active: Table movement prevented by interlock with I 72.3
   Safety: Material stays locked in place during active cutting

‚ö†Ô∏è I 73.7 (V/S Velocity Signal) - 10 REFERENCES
   Purpose: Master enable/gate for ALL motion
   When = 0: System enters safe stop (all motors stop)
   When = 1: Motion allowed if other conditions met
   Safety: Loss of signal = emergency stop equivalent

================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

‚úì I 72.3 is MOST CRITICAL (23 uses) - Table lock during cutting
‚úì I 73.7 is MASTER GATE - Controls ALL motion enable/disable
‚úì I 72.7 works with I 72.3 - Prevent cutting hazard (material shift)
‚úì System controls 5 independent axes (selected via I 73.0-73.4)
‚úì Speed applied globally to all axes (I 72.0 or I 72.1)
‚úì Direction selected per axis (I 73.5 or I 73.6)
‚úì Safety interlocks prevent operator hazards
‚úì Complex signal dependencies require careful integration
